A rare 50+ acre estate where vineyard, golf, and resort living meet.

THE OFFERING

Set across 50 plus acres and four separate legal parcels in the heart of a prestigious wine country AVA, Pebble is a one-of-a-kind estate blending world-class viticulture, luxurious living, and resort-style recreation — a private world of vineyards, golf, and gathering places, minutes from the best of Napa Valley and within easy reach of San Francisco.

The Clubhouse

Reimagined in a full 2022 remodel by award winning architect and interior designers, the 6,400-square-foot clubhouse is modern farmhouse at its finest — vaulted white-oak ceilings, walls of glass, and an open plan that flows from great room to bar to a heritage-oak deck. A full commercial kitchen, custom bar, and seating for up to 70 make it built for everything from intimate dinners to corporate retreats.

The Vineyard

Thirty-three acres of vineyard sit in the heart of the Los Carneros AVA, planted to Pinot Noir and Chardonnay and producing exceptional fruit year after year. The neighbors say plenty: Domaine Carneros, Artesa, and Cuvaison all farm the same celebrated ground.

Golf & Recreation

Pebble is one of Napa's true rarities — a private estate with its own golf experience. First built in 1960's, the course winds across the grounds with multiple tee boxes and challenging greens framed by vineyard views. Beyond the fairway: a resort pool and tennis pavilion, BBQ and picnic areas, a stocked fishing pond and dock, two lakes, and miles of walking paths.

The Residence

The 7,521-square-foot main residence and separate 1,500-square-foot guest house offer refined, light-filled living — high ceilings, valley views, and a seamless indoor-outdoor rhythm. Beautifully landscaped gardens, a fruit orchard, and a citrus grove complete the setting.

Pool & Amenities

A resort-scale pool anchors the grounds, with shaded cabanas, a tennis court, and brick terraces made for long Napa afternoons.

Location

In the heart of Los Carneros — the cool, breezy crossroads of Napa and Sonoma — Pebble is minutes from downtown Napa and the valley's finest wineries, and within convenient reach of San Francisco and the greater Bay Area.
